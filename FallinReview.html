Fall's FRESH List

Your Guide to the Best in Recent Music

The phenomenon that is the summer song is an interesting one. Some may say there’s a formula for a summer song: make it catchy, give it lots of synths, just have it talk about sex and drugs. Whatever the cynic inside you may want to build up as cliché these so called summer songs I’ve never been able to define a summer song. Instead it’s a feeling to me. It just fits. You’re in your car on a warm and sunny day and your music has never encapsulated you’re good vibrations quite like this. That bliss is highly contagious. It’s that same bliss that you can’t help but shout the lyrics alone in your car. Making this list was not easy. In an especially strong year for all music, the supply of summer jams has been ceaseless.

Albums

Certified FRESH

Mac Miller

GO:OD AM

>Like on Future’s stellar DS2, Travis Scott's Rodeo shows traps ability to craft an atmosphere as intriguing and mysterious as any other genre. Through the manipulation of vocals in a way that makes them a part of the production not a separate entity, Travis Scott creates melodies as catchy as they are drunkenly faded. Antidote matches the codeine blur of Future’s trap vision and is a natural late night banger. On the albums best track, 90201, the melding of his voice and production is especially evident. A plodding beat and contained singing opens up into an expanse of melodic trap bliss with Travis spitting his best flow. It would be impossible to not mention the impressive feature list Rodeo has assembled. Surprisingly Piss on Your Grave, his aggressive collaboration with Kanye, is the weakest of the bunch. Thankfully though that feature is the exception here. Both Young Thug and Justin Biebers features on Mariah I’m Drunk are the perfect complements to Travis’ druged pop sensibilities and elsewhere the guests are an interesting complement. Rodeo isn’t the most consistent album from start to finish but when his ideas work Travis Scott is just as interesting as any name in trap and electronic influenced rap.
